KineLoop

Date: Junuary, 22

Duration: '15

The Kineloop project is based around a music with a storytelling rhythm and a pre-existing color scheme.

It presents the basics of 3D animation: object deformation, transformation and the use of dynamics.

This project aims to demonstrate the misconception that you need a complex 3D scene to have an attention-grabbing rendering.

Here the color is seen as a main tool to bring out the volumes.

The trick was to play with the repetition of colors by creating a pattern for each group of objects.

The camera system had to maintain consistency between shots and time remapping, follow the rhythm of the music through the motion system.
